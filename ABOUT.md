## Bio

I'm a Design Executive, systems thinker, and builder with 20+ years of experience shaping consumer and enterprise product ecosystems across fintech, commerce, AI, and emerging ventures. My work combines industrial design, UX, and organizational strategy to create products, teams, and systems that scale with clarity, craft, and measurable business impact.

I've led large-scale transformations at Intuit and Walmart, helped early-stage ventures shape product direction and design capability, and founded ventures including Moongazing and a stealth-mode startup. I also convened and led #D4, Intuit India's design conference, helping build a strong design community around the philosophy of "Design for Delight."

I've had the privilege of leading design for India operations at major companies (Intuit, 247.ai, Walmart), a role that extends beyond typical design leadership. It meant:

- **Building world-class design capability from the ground up** — scaling from 17 to 62 designers while maintaining quality and culture
- **Establishing design as a viable career path in India's tech industry** — creating opportunities that didn't exist before
- **Positioning Indian design talent to leadership at global headquarters** — ensuring great designers from India could advance without relocating
- **Creating sustainable talent pipelines** — building programs that continue to attract and develop designers long after my tenure

This work—growing teams while building emerging market design centers and managing across geographies—has been foundational to how I think about organizational growth and cross-cultural leadership.
