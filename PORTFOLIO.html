About

I'm a Design Executive, systems thinker, and builder with 20+ years of experience shaping consumer and enterprise product ecosystems across fintech, commerce, AI, and emerging ventures. My work combines industrial design, UX, and organizational strategy to create products, teams, and systems that scale with clarity, craft, and measurable business impact.

I've led large-scale transformations at Intuit and Walmart, helped early-stage ventures shape product direction and design capability, and founded ventures including Moongazing and a stealth-mode startup. I also convened and led #D4, Intuit India's design conference, helping build a strong design community around the philosophy of "Design for Delight."

I'm particularly interested in the intersection of human behavior, systems thinking, AI-native experiences, and timeless design — translating complexity into intuitive products, scalable operations, and enduring design culture.
